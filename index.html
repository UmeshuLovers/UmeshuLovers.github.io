The Forest of Liars is a vast, lush labyrinth known for its ethereal beauty, but no one who has ventured in has ever returned. According to legend, the forest’s heart hides countless mysteries and uncharted places that defy the imagination.

Forest of Liars is an adventure game that delivers a different experience every time you play. You will encounter strange and extraordinary characters who can assist you on your quest to reach the center of the resplendent and dazzling wood.

This game is intended for PC, Mac, and consoles.
